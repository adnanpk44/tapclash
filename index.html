How to Play

1

Choose Your Mode

Play locally with a friend or challenge players online with Google Sign-In

2

Tap Fast

Tap your half of the screen as fast as possible. Watch your TPS (Taps Per Second) counter in real-time

3

Beat Your Opponent

Whoever gets the most taps before time runs out wins! Ties go to the last tap timestamp

4

Climb the Leaderboard

Track your highest TPS score globally and compete to be #1. Rematch your rivals and build streaks
